What's CHAIMASTER?

CHAINMASTER is a website dedicated to chainsaw culture.
It's set in a post-apocalytic universe, in a future where the world has been dragged into individual madness.

Additional Content

The chainsaw was invented in 1830 by Bernhard Heine as a medical tool. It was adapted for felling trees in 1926 by Andreas Stihl.
